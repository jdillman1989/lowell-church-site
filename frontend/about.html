About

American Liberty Insurance was founded in 2004 by a team of insurance professionals with over 40 years of collective experience in the industry. Our primary goal is to provide innovative workers' compensation coverage to employers who demand more than their current insurer can provide, and who are excited about the prospect of an insurer targeted at their individual needs.

For too long businesses have had too little to say in their Workers' Compensation coverage. American Liberty Insurance Company is here to provide an attractive alternative to the rigid, costly environment that employers have thus far tolerated from their insurers.
